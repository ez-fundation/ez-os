# EZ-OS Companion: The "Nano Banana" Character Design Bible

> **Project**: EZ-OS  
> **Subject**: Procedural Companion (Mascot)  
> **Design Philosophy**: "Retro-Futuristic Tamagotchi meets Industrial Design"

---

## üé® Visual Concept

The companion is a physical manifestation of digital memory. It is not just a character; it is a **container of history**.

### **Core Aesthetics**
- **Form Factor**: Dense, tactile, handheld. Think high-quality industrial plastic from the 90s (Braun, Nintendo, Sony).
- **Proportions**: "Chibi" mechanical. Big head/body block, tiny stabilizing limbs.
- **Texture**: Matte finish for the chassis, high-gloss for the screen faceplate.
- **Vibe**: Helpful, neutral, observant to compliant.

---

## üìê 3D Printing Specification

This design is optimized for FDM printing with minimal supports.

### **Geometry Anatomy**
1.  **Chassis (Body)**:
    -   30mm¬≥ Rounded Cube (Chamfered Box).
    -   Corner Radius: 2.5mm (Soft but technical).
    -   Surface: Matte texture.

2.  **Faceplate (Screen)**:
    -   Recessed 0.5mm into the front face.
    -   Material: Glossy Black/Green insert.
    -   Display: Pixel grid (simulated or real).

3.  **Antenna (Signal)**:
    -   Position: Top Right.
    -   Structure: Rigid stalks, spherical tip.
    -   Color: RAL 3020 (Traffic Red).

4.  **Limbs (Mobility)**:
    -   Legs: Cylindrical stubs (stability).
    -   Arms: None (canonical) or floating spheres (evolutionary).

## ü§ñ Procedural Evolution (The "Ghost in the Shell")

The physical print is the "Shell". The "Ghost" (personality) evolves procedurally.

### **Evolutionary Stages**

| Stage | Visual Cues | Unlock Condition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1: Base** | Clean grey chassis, neutral face. | Start |
| **Stage 2: Engaged** | Color shift (Green/Blue tint), expressive eyes. | 10 Sessions |
| **Stage 3: Veteran** | Weathered texture, "Hacker" accessories (hat/glasses). | 100 Hours |
